BENGALI FOOD

Vegetarian Dishes

Bengali cuisine offers a variety of delicious vegetarian dishes made with fresh vegetables and spices. Here are some popular Bengali vegetarian dishes:

  • Aloo Posto
  • Shukto
  • Mochar Ghonto
  • Labra
  • Bhaja Moonger Dal
  • Chorchori
  • Begun Bhaja

Non-Vegetarian Dishes

Bengali cuisine also includes a variety of flavorful non-vegetarian dishes, especially seafood and meat-based preparations. Here are some popular Bengali non-vegetarian dishes:

  • Macher Jhol
  • Chingri Malai Curry
  • Kosha Mangsho
  • Doi Maach
  • Murighonto
  • Chitol Maacher Muitha
  • Chicken Rezala
